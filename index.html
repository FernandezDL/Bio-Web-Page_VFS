About Me

My name is Diana Lucía Fernández Villatoro, I'm 22 years old, I'm from Guatemala and I'm the youngest of 4 siblings.

I participated in a game jam in may and I won with my game “Conversation on the sofa” and that's why I'm here at VFS now. It's been difficult being away from my family but I'm determined at graduating from VFS with the best possible grade.

I like to hear and play music, I play the piano since 2016. Also I grew up in a little town called Mazatenango and I live in a farm, so since I was little I enjoyed riding horses, and since last year I practiced it at a higher level, even starting to jump obstacles

I have been working as a developer for the past 2 years, first just as a Front-end dev and then as a Full-stack developer. Now I like to take my developing skills to the next level and try to be a Game developer
